In action

One prompt. The whole picture.

Understanding & Research

"I want an explainer for how blizzards happen, in context of the ongoing one in NYC. Show the forecast, the build-up, the path across the state. Use metric. Light colors like snow."

One prompt. You get a full briefing — the science behind the storm, a timeline of events, formation diagrams you can actually read, info cards breaking down key facts, and a live forecast chart with temperature, snowfall, and wind data.

  • Visual explainers
  • Live forecasts
  • Key stats at a glance
  • Deep-dive sections
Tracking & Habits

"Log my runs and actually see improvement"

That was the first prompt. Then: "make it flexible for any activity." Now it's a full tracker — dashboard, activity logging, history, stats over time. Log sessions with one message, come back tomorrow and pick up where you left off.

  • Log in one line
  • Stats over time
  • Searchable history
  • Always saved
